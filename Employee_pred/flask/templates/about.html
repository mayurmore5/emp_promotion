About the Employee Promotion Prediction Model

This project predicts the likelihood of an employee being promoted based on department, trainings, previous-year rating, length of service, KPI achievement, awards, and average training score.

Key analysis and findings:

  • Previous year's rating, length of service, and average training score strongly correlate with promotion.
  • Recognitions (awards) and certain departments are associated with higher promotion rates.
  • Model used: XGBoost classifier with strong accuracy, precision, recall, and F1-score.

Use this app to identify high-potential employees and guide development programs.
